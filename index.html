About Me:

I am a Research Scientist specializing in Computer Vision, Human-Computer Interaction, and Extended Reality (XR). My research focuses on gaze analysis, vision-language models (VLMs), video understanding, and mixed reality applications. I develop intelligent systems that perceive and interpret human behavior from both egocentric and third-person perspectives. My work bridges academic research and applied innovation, targeting industrial environments, cultural heritage, and human performance enhancement through XR technologies. Currently, I am a Senior Computer Vision Scientist at Next Vision, where I contribute to cutting-edge projects integrating visual AI, wearable sensing, and immersive technologies.
